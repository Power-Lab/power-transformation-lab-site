Wendy is a third-year undergraduate student at UC San Diego majoring in Data Science with a minor in Economics. At the Power Transformation Lab, she serves as a Student Web Designer, creating interactive dashboards to make complex energy data more accessible. She is passionate about using human-centered design and data visualization to translate technical climate research into actionable insights for a general audience. She enjoys hiking, painting, and exploring new food spots with friends in her free time.
