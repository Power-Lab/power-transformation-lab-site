Lauren is a fourth-year undergraduate student at UC San Diego majoring in Data Science. At the Power Transformation Lab, she works on data collection, cleaning, analysis, and visualization to support research on deploying low-carbon energy systems at scale. She is passionate about using data science and spatial analysis to inform just and effective climate and energy policies that improve outcomes for underserved communities. Her interests lie in using data-driven methods to inform climate and energy policy, and in building tools that make complex energy systems more understandable to decision-makers and communities. In her free time, she enjoys backpacking, cooking, and photography.
